Our Mission

Inspired by Numbers 6:24, Twenty Four Roses is a brand rooted in the belief that beauty products should not only enhance but also uplift your daily routine. We specialize in luxurious hair and skin care offerings designed to delight your senses and nourish your body.

Our Products

  • Hair Perfume: Enchanting fragrances for all hair types, with oil-based formulas designed to leave your hair radiant and refreshed.
  • All-Over Body Butter: A versatile moisturizer enriched with shea butter and marula oil to deeply hydrate hair and skin.
